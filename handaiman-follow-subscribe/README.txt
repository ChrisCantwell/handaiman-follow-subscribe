Clean podcast subscription and social follow links for TheHandAIMan.

== Description ==

HandAIMan Follow & Subscribe provides configurable podcast subscription and social follow links without vendor accounts, tracking widgets, or external JavaScript.

Shortcodes:

[handaiman_follow]
[ha_follow]
[handaiman_podcast_links]
[handaiman_social_links]

Useful examples:

[handaiman_follow collapsed="yes"]
[handaiman_follow layout="compact"]
[handaiman_follow podcast="yes" social="no"]
[handaiman_follow platforms="rss,episodes,youtube"]
